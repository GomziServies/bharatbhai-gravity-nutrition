Product label information

GRAVITY Nutrition Super Whey Mawa Kulfi is a mawa kulfi-flavoured whey protein blend designed to provide essential support for muscle growth and recovery. This formula features 24g of protein, 6.1g of BCAAs, and 6.4g of EAAs per serving, ensuring a comprehensive nutrient profile for athletes and fitness enthusiasts. The detailed label panel covers ingredients, nutrition facts, amino acid profile, directions, and storage guidance.

Ingredients

Whey Protein Concentrate, Milk Solids, Monk Fruit Extract, Artificial flavour, Glutamine, Emulsifier [INS 322(i)], Thickener [INS 415], Digestive Enzyme Blend.

Label notes

  • Allergen advice: This product contains lactose and is not recommended for those with lactose intolerance.
  • Taste advice: For a thick, creamy and delicious shake, use nonfat milk or skimmed milk; nuts can be added as per calorie intake.
  • Recommended duration of use: Use the product for at least 30 days for effective results or as suggested by your healthcare professional.
  • Recommended use: Two serving per day.
  • Storage: Store under 25°C temperature, dry and hygienic place. Keep away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.
  • Caution: Consult a healthcare practitioner prior to use if you are pregnant or you have liver disease or if you've been instructed to follow a low-protein diet. Not to exceed the recommended daily usage.

DIRECTIONS TO USE

Put 1 scoop in 220-250ml of water or milk, shake and stir until it dissolves completely, and the shake is ready to be consumed.
